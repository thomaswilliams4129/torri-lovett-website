Hey friends, Torri here!

A little bit about myself, I’m a Jesus follower who is so incredibly grateful to serve a Savior who is the Author and Perfecter of our hope. I constantly find myself in awe of His creation in the world and people. Part of the reason I love photography is that I get to capture just that.

I am an enneagram type 2 which means it is so fulfilling for me to love and encourage everyone around me! I adore design and styling and have for as long as I can remember, so don’t be afraid to ask me questions about what to wear or help come up with decorative aspects of your wedding! Those sort of things makes my heart oh so full!

In my sessions, I want it to be moments that express who YOU are. These are YOUR pictures and they should be unique to YOU. If you want maternity pictures on a jet ski, or senior pictures in a cemetery, or engagement pictures where you had your first date (aka cfa) then let’s make it happen. I want to team up with your creative thoughts and ideas and express the person you were made to be. I’m here for the big moments and the little details. I don’t want to just take pretty pictures. I want to create beautifully unique moments and capture them for you to keep for a lifetime.

Thanks for checking out my website, browse around some more if you’d like!
